We are UTC's Computer Machinery & Computer Science club! 
We hold regular meetings where we talk about exciting computing concepts and get our hands dirty 
with a variety of club projects. This is the best place to come get to know other CS majors
and get experience in the broad field of computer science! We hold workshops on using GitHub, 
introduction to new programming languages, explore cutting edge computer science news, and much more!
