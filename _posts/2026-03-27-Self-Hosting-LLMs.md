Today, Guest Speaker Justice Peltier gave a talk on hosting LLMs on your own local device!
He gave us an intro on benefits of self-hosting, different self-hosting methods (bare metal and containerized), models and model runners, adding a GUI, and some ways you put your local models to use.

He also gave us a demo of different open models, such as Ollama, DeepSeek, and HuggingFace models.

If you would like an overview of the talk, please see Justice's [GitHub Repository](https://github.com/tPeltier/talk_to_yourself), which has a [guide to self hosting LLMs](https://github.com/tPeltier/talk_to_yourself/blob/main/talk_to_yourself.md) and other helpful resources!
